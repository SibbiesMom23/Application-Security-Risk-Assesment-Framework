# HIPAA to NIST 800-53 Mapping (Starter Example)

This document provides a simple, high-level example of how selected HIPAA Security Rule safeguards can be mapped to NIST SP 800-53 controls. It is not a complete or official mapping, but a practical reference for risk and compliance work.

---

## Administrative Safeguards

### HIPAA: 164.308(a)(1) – Security Management Process

- Description:
  Implement policies and procedures to prevent, detect, contain, and correct security violations.

- Example NIST 800-53 Controls:
  - RA-1: Risk Assessment Policy and Procedures  
  - RA-3: Risk Assessment  
  - RA-5: Vulnerability Monitoring and Scanning  
  - SI-2: Flaw Remediation  
  - CA-2: Control Assessments  

---

### HIPAA: 164.308(a)(3) – Workforce Security

- Description:
  Ensure appropriate access for workforce members and prevent unauthorized access.

- Example NIST 800-53 Controls:
  - AC-2: Account Management  
  - AC-3: Access Enforcement  
  - AC-5: Separation of Duties  
  - AC-6: Least Privilege  
  - PS-2: Position Risk Designation  

---

### HIPAA: 164.308(a)(5) – Security Awareness and Training

- Description:
  Implement a security training program for all workforce members.

- Example NIST 800-53 Controls:
  - AT-1: Awareness and Training Policy and Procedures  
  - AT-2: Literacy and Awareness Training  
  - AT-3: Role-Based Training  

---

## Technical Safeguards

### HIPAA: 164.312(a)(1) – Access Control

- Description:
  Implement technical controls to allow access only to authorized users.

- Example NIST 800-53 Controls:
  - AC-2: Account Management  
  - AC-3: Access Enforcement  
  - AC-7: Unsuccessful Logon Attempts  
  - AC-17: Remote Access  
  - IA-2: Identification and Authentication  

---

### HIPAA: 164.312(c)(1) – Integrity

- Description:
  Protect ePHI from improper alteration or destruction.

- Example NIST 800-53 Controls:
  - SC-8: Transmission Confidentiality and Integrity  
  - SI-7: Information Integrity  
  - AU-2: Logging  
  - AU-6: Audit Review  

---

### HIPAA: 164.312(e)(1) – Transmission Security

- Description:
  Guard against unauthorized access to ePHI in transit.

- Example NIST 800-53 Controls:
  - SC-8: Transmission Confidentiality and Integrity  
  - SC-12: Key Management  
  - SC-13: Cryptographic Protection
